## Applies to all documents

### Properties
- No tags

### Headings
- Use headings only when grouping distinct topics aids fast scanning: not as boilerplate structure
- Do not add fixed headings (e.g. "Summary", "Notes") unless the content actually requires that separation
- `##` is the largest heading level

### Body
- Bullet points preferred for factual, list-style content
- Keep entries brief and direct, no filler or padding

### Punctuation
- No em dashes
